OUR STORY

Technology has fueled the exponential growth of industries. Agents of Social change have rarely harnessed this power. We realised the path to sustainable growth lied at the intersection of tech and social good.


Hack for Social Good is our initiative to leverage emerging tech and solve for Global Goals. To piece together an ecosystem for problem seekers and solution providers.
